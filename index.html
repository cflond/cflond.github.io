About Me

Charles started to use a computer when he was just a child. He built his first website when he was just 13 years old. He learned by himself website development and did his first professional website at 16 for the city where he lived.

In order to reinforce his knowledge, Charles decided to go to an IT Engineering School in Paris, the ECE. He choose an international curriculum with semesters in Canada & USA. He selected a special focus on Internet for Business. He was valedictorian of his promotion. During his studies Charles did many internships all related to the web with intranet & big data projects.

As a first job, Charles joined Jahia Solutions Group, a worldwide software vendor of CMS / Portal solutions. He was consultant for major customers such as E. Leclerc, MACIF, European Parlement, ... He then moved to a position of Senior Consultant at Fredhopper, the leader of searchandising solutions for e-commerce websites. He worked for the bigest players in Europe such as Vente-Priv√©e, ASOS, Boulanger, Sarenza and others. Charles then started a new challenge at Akamai Technologies, the company who makes the web fast, reliable and secure. As a Technical Account Manager he was responsible for some strategic accounts such as France Televisions or BNP Paribas.

With experience around open source, search, distributed systems & cloud, the next natural move for Charles was to join Elastic, the company behind the Elastic Stack (Elasticsearch, Kibana, Logstash, Beats). He helped to boostrap the business in EMEA-S as well as do build the customer success practice globally. After almost 3 years and a successful IPO, it was time for Charles to look for his next adventure. Lately, he has joined Tanium, a revolutionary endpoint management scale-up to help them estabilishing business in France as well as developping the customer success practice.
